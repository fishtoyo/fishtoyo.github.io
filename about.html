關於我

擁有16年以上實務經驗

自 13 歲製作第一個網站起，這份熱愛已持續 26 年，早已內化成我生活的一部分。
憑藉這份初衷，我一路攻讀資料處理與資訊管理專業，並正式投入網頁開發領域 16 年。

我始終將不斷進化的技術視為養分，從早期的 jQuery、Bootstrap 到現代的 Vue 與 AI 驅動開發，我享受每一場技術變革帶來的可能性。

在累積 290+ 個專案的過程中，我磨練出對跨裝置適應性（RWD）的精準掌控。

不論是 活動網站、購物車平台、企業形象網站，或是 舊站 RWD 重構，我都具備豐富經驗與實作成果。對於 跨瀏覽器、跨裝置的適應性 以及 人性化的互動設計，亦有成熟的理解與應用。

網站設計對我而言不只是專業，更是結合熱情與創造力的持續探索。

My passion for web development ignited at age 13, a spark that has endured for 26 years and become an intrinsic part of who I am. Building on this lifelong interest, I pursued formal education in Data Processing and Information Management, eventually leading to a 16-year career as a professional developer delivering over 280 projects.

I view the constant evolution of technology as nourishment for my craft. From the early days of jQuery and Bootstrap to modern Vue.js and AI-driven development, I embrace the possibilities brought by every technological shift. Through the delivery of over 280+ projects, I have honed a precise mastery over Responsive Web Design (RWD) and cross-device compatibility.

My portfolio encompasses a wide range of achievements, including promotional microsites, e-commerce platforms, corporate identities, and legacy site RWD reconstructions. I possess a mature understanding and practical application of cross-browser consistency, adaptive layouts, and user-centric interactive design.

To me, web design is not just a profession; it is a continuous exploration that combines passion, creativity, and technical excellence.
